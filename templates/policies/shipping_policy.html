🌐 International shipping

We're happy to serve customers around the world. Please note the following for international orders:

  • • Customs & duties: Import taxes, customs duties, and fees may be levied by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in our shipping costs.
  • • Delivery delays: International shipments may be subject to customs clearance delays, which are beyond our control.
  • • Tracking: All international orders include tracking. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.
  • • Restricted items: Some items may be restricted in certain countries. We reserve the right to cancel orders that cannot be shipped due to local regulations.

📍 Where we ship

We currently ship to all 50 US states, plus over 50 international destinations. If your country isn't listed at checkout, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Note: We do not ship to PO boxes or APO/FPO addresses at this time.

📦 Order tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order directly from your account dashboard.

It may take 24–48 hours for tracking information to appear on the carrier's website.

⚠️ Lost or damaged packages

If your package arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos. For lost packages, we'll work with the carrier to locate it or issue a replacement/refund after the estimated delivery date has passed.

🎉 Holiday shipping

During peak seasons (Black Friday, Christmas, etc.), delivery times may be longer than usual due to carrier volume. We recommend ordering early to ensure timely delivery.

Check our Holiday Shipping Deadlines page for specific cutoff dates.
