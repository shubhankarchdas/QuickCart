Return policy

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, our 30-day return policy makes it easy to send items back for a refund or exchange.

You have 30 days from delivery to initiate a return. Most items qualify for free return shipping.

💰

Full refund

Once we receive your return, we'll issue a refund to your original payment method within 5 business days.

🔄

Easy exchanges

🏷️

Final sale items

Some marked-down items are final sale and cannot be returned. Check product page for details.

How to return an item

Follow these simple steps to initiate your return:

Log into your account

Go to "My Orders" and find the order containing the item you wish to return. Click "Return Items".

Select items & reason

Choose which items you're returning and select a reason. This helps us improve.

Print return label

Download and print your prepaid return shipping label. Pack your items securely.

Ship & track

Drop off at any carrier location. You'll receive email updates when we process your return.

💡 No account? You can also initiate a return by contacting our support team at returns@quickcart.com with your order number.

Return conditions

To ensure a smooth return process, please note the following requirements:

Condition Status Notes
Item unworn/unwashed ✓ Required Items must be in original condition with no signs of wear, stains, or odor.
Original tags attached ✓ Required All tags must be intact and attached to the garment.
Original packaging ⚠️ Recommended Please use the original packaging if possible, or any sturdy box/envelope.
Return window ✓ 30 days Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery date.
Receipt/invoice ✓ Included Include the packing slip or a note with your order number inside the package.

🚫 Non-returnable items

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons:

  • • Intimates & lingerie (unless sealed)
  • • Swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken)
  • • Earrings & jewelry
  • • Gift cards
  • • Final sale items (marked clearly)
  • • Customized or personalized items

💵 Refund methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • • Credit/debit cards: 5–10 business days
  • • PayPal: 2–3 business days
  • • Razorpay/UPI: 3–5 business days
  • • Store credit: Instant (if you choose this option)

You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed.

🌍 International returns

For customers outside the United States:

Return shipping

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or incorrect.

Customs & duties

We cannot refund any customs duties or taxes paid. Please check with your local customs office.

Processing time

International returns may take up to 15 business days to reach us. Refunds will be processed upon receipt.

Common return questions

What if I received a defective item?

We're sorry! Please contact us immediately with photos, and we'll provide a prepaid return label and issue a full refund or replacement.

Can I return a gift?

Yes, but refunds will be issued as store credit to the gift recipient unless the original payment method is provided.

Do I need the original box?

No, any sturdy box or envelope works. Just ensure items are well-protected and include your order number.
