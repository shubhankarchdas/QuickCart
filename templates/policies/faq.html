Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about ordering, shipping, returns, and more.

🛍️ Orders (6 questions)

How do I place an order? +
Simply browse our collection, select your size and quantity, and click "Add to Cart." Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, enter your shipping and payment details, and confirm your order. You'll receive a confirmation email shortly.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it? +
We process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to make changes, contact us within 1 hour of placing your order at support@quickcart.com. After that, we may not be able to modify it, but you can always return items for a refund.
Will I receive an order confirmation? +
Yes! Once your order is successfully placed, you'll receive an email confirmation with your order number and details. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact us.
How do I track my order? +
After your order ships, we'll send you a tracking link via email. You can also log into your account dashboard and view your order history for real-time tracking updates.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Razorpay for secure online payments.
Is it safe to use my credit card on your site? +
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with trusted payment gateways like Razorpay. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

🚚 Shipping & Delivery (5 questions)

How much does shipping cost? +
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, shipping is a flat rate of $4.99. Express shipping is available starting at $12.99.
How long will it take to receive my order? +
Standard shipping typically takes 3–7 business days within the US. Express shipping takes 1–3 business days. International shipping may take 7–14 business days depending on customs.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see the exact cost at checkout.
Will I have to pay customs fees or import duties? +
International orders may be subject to customs fees, duties, or taxes imposed by your country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer. Please check with your local customs office for more information.
What if my package is lost or stolen? +
If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received it, contact us immediately. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue. For lost packages, we'll either reship or issue a refund.

🔄 Returns & Refunds (5 questions)

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. Some final sale items are not eligible for return.
How do I initiate a return? +
Log into your account, go to your orders, and click "Return Items." Follow the instructions to print a return label. You can also contact our support team for assistance.
Do I have to pay for return shipping? +
For customers in the US, return shipping is free for most items. For international returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or incorrect.
How long do refunds take? +
Once we receive your return, we process refunds within 3–5 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take another 2–5 business days to appear.
Can I exchange an item instead of returning? +
Yes! For exchanges, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures you get the correct size/color as quickly as possible.

💳 Payments (4 questions)

Which payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Razorpay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Do you offer installment payments or BNPL? +
Yes, through Razorpay we offer "Buy Now, Pay Later" options like Klarna and Affirm at checkout for eligible orders. You can split your payment into interest-free installments.
Is my payment information secure? +
Absolutely. We use SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. Your payment details are processed directly by our payment partners; we never see or store your full card information.
Why was my payment declined? +
Declines can happen for various reasons: insufficient funds, incorrect CVV, or bank security blocks. Try another payment method or contact your bank. If the issue persists, our support team can help.

👤 Account (4 questions)

How do I create an account? +
Click on "My Account" at the top of the page and select "Register." Enter your email and create a password. You can also sign up using Google for faster checkout.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it? +
On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder.
How do I update my profile or change my email? +
Log into your account and go to the "Profile" section. You can edit your personal information, change your email, upload a profile picture, and update saved addresses.
Can I delete my account? +
Yes. Please contact our support team at support@quickcart.com with your account details, and we'll assist you in deleting your account and associated data.

👗 Product Info (5 questions)

How do I find my correct size? +
Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements. You can also refer to our general size chart in the footer. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit.
Are your clothes true to size? +
Most of our items are designed to fit true to size based on standard measurements. However, certain styles may have a relaxed or tailored fit – this is noted in the product description.
What fabrics do you use? +
We prioritize natural and sustainable fibers like organic cotton, linen, Tencel, and recycled polyester. Each product page lists the fabric composition and care instructions.
How do I care for my items? +
Care instructions are included on the product page and on the garment's label. Generally, we recommend gentle washing in cold water and air drying to extend the life of your clothes.
Are your products sustainably made? +
We're committed to sustainability. We work with ethical manufacturers, use eco-friendly materials where possible, and continuously reduce our carbon footprint. Check our "Our Story" page for more details.
